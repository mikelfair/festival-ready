Frequently Asked Questions

What should I include in my director's biography?

Focus on your filmmaking journey, relevant education, any film festival awards or recognition, previous film projects, and your unique artistic vision. Keep it professional yet personal.

How long should my synopsis be?

Aim for 100-250 words that capture your story's core conflict, main characters, and emotional journey. Be clear, compelling, and include the ending.

What's the difference between statements and biographies?

Statements focus on your specific project (why you created this film/screenplay), while biographies focus on your career and experience as a filmmaker or writer.

Should I tailor materials for each festival?

Yes! Research each festival's focus and values. A comedy festival wants to hear about your humor approach, while a social justice festival cares about your film's impact.
